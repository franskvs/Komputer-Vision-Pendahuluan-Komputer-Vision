## A. BUKU REFERENSI (10 Buku)

### Buku Utama

1. **Szeliski, R.** (2022). *Computer Vision: Algorithms and Applications*, 2nd Edition. Springer. Chapter 10: Computational Photography.
   - Referensi utama untuk materi ini
   - Cakupan: HDR, tone mapping, image matting, flash photography
   - Online: https://szeliski.org/Book/

2. **Reinhard, E., Heidrich, W., Debevec, P., Pattanaik, S., Ward, G., & Myszkowski, K.** (2010). *High Dynamic Range Imaging: Acquisition, Display, and Image-Based Lighting*, 2nd Edition. Morgan Kaufmann.
   - Comprehensive HDR reference
   - Cakupan: HDR capture, tone mapping, display technology
   - ISBN: 978-0123749147

3. **Raskar, R. & Tumblin, J.** (2009). *Computational Photography: Mastering New Techniques for Lenses, Lighting, and Sensors*. A K Peters/CRC Press.
   - Foundational computational photography text
   - Cakupan: Coded aperture, light field, HDR
   - ISBN: 978-1568813134

### Buku Pendukung

4. **Gonzalez, R. C. & Woods, R. E.** (2018). *Digital Image Processing*, 4th Edition. Pearson.
   - Image processing fundamentals
   - Cakupan: Enhancement, restoration, color processing
   - ISBN: 978-0133356724

5. **Burger, W. & Burge, M. J.** (2022). *Digital Image Processing: An Algorithmic Introduction Using Java*, 3rd Edition. Springer.
   - Practical implementation focus
   - Cakupan: Filters, transformations, morphology
   - ISBN: 978-3030563394

6. **Paris, S., Kornprobst, P., Tumblin, J., & Durand, F.** (2009). *Bilateral Filtering: Theory and Applications*. Foundations and Trends in Computer Graphics and Vision.
   - Deep dive into bilateral filtering
   - Cakupan: Theory, applications, extensions
   - DOI: 10.1561/0600000020

7. **Adelson, E. H.** (2000). *The Plenoptic Function and the Elements of Early Vision*. MIT Press.
   - Light field theory
   - Cakupan: Plenoptic function, computational optics
   - Fundamental for understanding light field photography

8. **Debevec, P. & Malik, J.** (2008). *Rendering Synthetic Objects into Real Scenes*. SIGGRAPH Course Notes.
   - HDR imaging fundamentals
   - Cakupan: Response curve recovery, radiance maps
   - Classic reference for HDR

9. **Buades, A., Coll, B., & Morel, J. M.** (2011). *Non-Local Means Denoising*. Image Processing On Line.
   - Comprehensive NLM reference
   - Cakupan: Theory, implementation, parameters
   - DOI: 10.5201/ipol.2011.bcm_nlm

10. **Fattal, R., Lischinski, D., & Werman, M.** (2002). *Gradient Domain High Dynamic Range Compression*. ACM SIGGRAPH.
    - Gradient domain processing
    - Cakupan: Tone mapping, Poisson editing
    - Classic paper on local tone mapping
