# Referensi Bab 14: Image-Based Rendering

## Buku Referensi

### 1. Szeliski, R. (2022)
**Computer Vision: Algorithms and Applications, 2nd Edition**
Springer. ISBN: 978-3-030-34371-2
- Chapter 14: Image-Based Rendering
- Comprehensive coverage of IBR spectrum
- Online: https://szeliski.org/Book/

### 2. Shum, H. Y., Chan, S. C., & Kang, S. B. (2007)
**Image-Based Rendering**
Springer. ISBN: 978-0-387-32668-8
- Definitive textbook on IBR
- Light fields, view interpolation, layered representations
- Theoretical foundations and practical algorithms

### 3. McMillan, L. (1997)
**An Image-Based Approach to Three-Dimensional Computer Graphics**
PhD Thesis, University of North Carolina
- Foundational work on DIBR
- Image warping theory
- 3D warping equation

### 4. Hartley, R. & Zisserman, A. (2004)
**Multiple View Geometry in Computer Vision, 2nd Edition**
Cambridge University Press. ISBN: 978-0-521-54051-3
- Homography and projective geometry
- Essential for understanding view transformations

### 5. Forsyth, D. A. & Ponce, J. (2012)
**Computer Vision: A Modern Approach, 2nd Edition**
Pearson. ISBN: 978-0-13-608592-8
- Chapter on image stitching
- Practical panorama algorithms

### 6. Mildenhall, B., et al. (2021)
**NeRF: Representing Scenes as Neural Radiance Fields for View Synthesis**
Communications of the ACM, 65(1), 99-106
- Seminal paper on Neural Radiance Fields
- Volume rendering dengan neural networks

### 7. Levoy, M. & Hanrahan, P. (1996)
**Light Field Rendering**
SIGGRAPH '96 Proceedings
- Original light field paper
- Plenoptic function parameterization

### 8. Gortler, S. J., et al. (1996)
**The Lumigraph**
SIGGRAPH '96 Proceedings
- Alternative light field representation
- Depth-corrected rendering

### 9. Brown, M. & Lowe, D. G. (2007)
**Automatic Panoramic Image Stitching using Invariant Features**
International Journal of Computer Vision, 74(1), 59-73
- State-of-the-art panorama stitching
- AutoStitch algorithm

### 10. Kerbl, B., et al. (2023)
**3D Gaussian Splatting for Real-Time Radiance Field Rendering**
ACM Transactions on Graphics (SIGGRAPH)
- Real-time neural rendering
- Explicit 3D Gaussian representation
