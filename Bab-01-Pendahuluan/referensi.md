## 📚 Buku Referensi

### Buku Utama
1. **Szeliski, R. (2022)**. *Computer Vision: Algorithms and Applications* (2nd ed.). Springer.
   - Website resmi: https://szeliski.org/Book
   - PDF gratis tersedia di website
   - Buku utama yang menjadi acuan materi ini

### Buku Pendukung
2. **Gonzalez, R. C., & Woods, R. E. (2018)**. *Digital Image Processing* (4th ed.). Pearson.
   - Fokus pada image processing
   - Penjelasan matematis yang mendalam
   
3. **Prince, S. J. D. (2012)**. *Computer Vision: Models, Learning, and Inference*. Cambridge University Press.
   - PDF gratis: http://www.computervisionmodels.com
   - Pendekatan probabilistik

4. **Forsyth, D. A., & Ponce, J. (2012)**. *Computer Vision: A Modern Approach* (2nd ed.). Pearson.
   - Cakupan komprehensif
   - Banyak contoh dan ilustrasi

5. **Hartley, R., & Zisserman, A. (2004)**. *Multiple View Geometry in Computer Vision* (2nd ed.). Cambridge University Press.
   - Untuk topik geometri 3D dan multiview
   - Standar referensi untuk geometric CV

## 📄 Paper Penting

### Paper Klasik
1. **Marr, D. (1982)**. *Vision: A Computational Investigation*
   - Fondasi teoretis computer vision
   
2. **Canny, J. (1986)**. "A Computational Approach to Edge Detection"
   - Algoritma deteksi tepi klasik

3. **Harris, C., & Stephens, M. (1988)**. "A Combined Corner and Edge Detector"
   - Harris corner detector

### Paper Modern (Deep Learning Era)
4. **Krizhevsky, A., Sutskever, I., & Hinton, G. E. (2012)**. "ImageNet Classification with Deep Convolutional Neural Networks"
   - AlexNet - memulai era deep learning
   
5. **He, K., Zhang, X., Ren, S., & Sun, J. (2016)**. "Deep Residual Learning for Image Recognition"
   - ResNet - arsitektur milestone

6. **LeCun, Y., Bengio, Y., & Hinton, G. (2015)**. "Deep Learning"
   - Review paper penting di Nature
